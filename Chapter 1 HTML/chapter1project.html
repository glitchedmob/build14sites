A few Facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is the largest of the terrestrial planets. Unlike the other planets in the solor system that are named after classic deities the Earth's name comes form the Anglo-Saxon woed erda which means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approximately 4054 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life.
